How to Use the Number Generator:

  1. Specify the minimum value in the 'Min' input box.

Random Name/Choice Picker

Having trouble making a decision? Our random name/choice picker can help. From selecting tonight's dinner to choosing a movie or even picking a travel destination, let randomness guide you.

The selected choice will appear here.

How to Use the Name/Choice Picker:

  1. Enter your list of names or choices, each on a new line in the provided text box.

  3. Your randomly selected choice will be displayed below.
